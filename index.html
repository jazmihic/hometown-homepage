Top three activities to do in Monroe

Ouachita River

The chief cities along the river are Arkadelphia and Camden, Ark., and Monroe, La. Formerly called the Washita River, the name Ouachita is derived from that of an Indian tribe.

Biedenharn Museum & Gardens

The Biedenharn Museum & Gardens is a museum complex that features regularly scheduled exhibits and a multitude of events.

Excalibur Family Fun Center

A covered walkway brings you to a 3,000-square-foot laser tag course. Outside you will find two go-kart tracks, batting cages and miniature golf.
